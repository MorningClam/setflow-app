Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 25, 2025

Welcome to Setflow! Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how Setflow ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("User" or "you") of the Setflow application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

Information We Collect

We may collect personal identification information from Users in various ways, including, but not limited to:

  • When Users register on the Service (name, email address).
  • When Users complete their profile (location, bio, links to social media/music platforms, profile picture).
  • When Users post content (gigs, gear listings, player posts, jam sessions, messages, reviews).
  • When Users interact with features (applying for gigs, booking artists, saving preferences).

We may also collect non-personal identification information whenever Users interact with our Service (e.g., browser name, device type, operating system).

How We Use Collected Information

Setflow uses Users' personal information for the following purposes:

  • To operate and personalize the Service: Displaying profiles, matching artists with gigs, showing relevant content.
  • To improve customer service: Information helps us respond to your support requests more efficiently.
  • To send periodic emails: We may use the email address to send User information and updates pertaining to their account or bookings. It may also be used to respond to inquiries or questions.
  • To facilitate communication: Enabling messaging between users.

How We Protect Your Information

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures (including data encryption and secure server practices) to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information, username, password, transaction information, and data stored on our Service. Our backend infrastructure is hosted on Google Cloud Platform / Firebase, leveraging their security measures.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent Users' personal identification information to others. Your profile information (name, location, bio, links, associated bands) is visible to other registered users as necessary for the functioning of the platform (e.g., when applying for a gig, browsing artists). Messages are private between participants. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners for the purposes outlined above. We only share information with third parties when required by law or to provide essential services (like payment processing, if implemented).

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with the Service and as described in this Privacy Policy. We will also retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can manage most of your profile information through your Account Settings within the app. For account deletion, please use the "Delete Account" option in settings, which will trigger our automated deletion process.
